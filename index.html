Origins in the Wild

How did the modern cat family evolve?

The cat family, Felidae, originated from a panther-like ancestor in Asia approximately 10.8 million years ago. Genetic evidence indicates this common ancestor evolved in Southeast Asia, leading to various cat lineages that adapted to diverse habitats and developed unique characteristics.

Over millions of years, these cats spread across continents due to migrations facilitated by land bridges formed by fluctuating sea levels.

Cat Breeds

The domestic cat, Felis catus, is a subspecies of the wildcat, Felis silvestris lybica.

Domestic cats have been selectively bred for thousands of years, resulting in a wide variety of breeds with distinct characteristics.

Today, there are over 40 recognized breeds, showcasing the adapdability and evolution of cats alongside human civilization.

Cute Cats of Today

Cats have become one of the most popular pets worldwide, known for their playful and affectionate nature.

The internet has played a significant role in the rise of cat culture, with viral cat videos and memes capturing the hearts of millions.

From Grumpy Cat to Lil Bub, these feline celebrities have become icons of the digital age, showing the enduring appeal of cats.

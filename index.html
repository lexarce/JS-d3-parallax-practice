Origins in the Wild

How did the modern cat family evolve?

The cat family, Felidae, originated from a panther-like ancestor in Asia approximately 10.8 million years ago. Genetic evidence indicates this common ancestor evolved in Southeast Asia, leading to various cat lineages that adapted to diverse habitats and developed unique characteristics.

Over millions of years, these cats spread across continents due to migrations facilitated by land bridges formed by fluctuating sea levels.
